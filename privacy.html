This Privacy Policy explains how [Company Legal Name] ("Jonah," "we," "us") collects, uses, stores, protects, and deletes information when you use the Jonah application and website (the "Service"). Jonah is an independent tool for Amazon sellers and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Amazon.

1. Who we are & how to contact us

The data controller for the Service is [Company Legal Name], [registered address]. For any privacy, data-deletion, or security request, contact us at support@getjonah.com. We respond to verified requests within 30 days.

2. Information we access from Amazon

With your explicit authorization (granted via Amazon's OAuth flow), Jonah accesses data through Amazon's official Selling Partner API (SP-API) and Amazon Ads API, limited to what is necessary to provide the Service:

  • Catalog & listing data — ASINs, SKUs, titles, attributes, images.
  • Pricing & offer data — your prices, Buy Box status, competitor offer summaries.
  • Sales & performance data — order counts, units, sessions, conversion metrics, search-query performance.
  • Inventory data — stock levels and velocity.
  • Advertising data — campaign spend, impressions, clicks, ACOS and related metrics via the Amazon Ads API.
  • Cost data you provide — unit costs, fees, and margin inputs you enter to enable profit calculations.
We do not request, access, or process shopper Personally Identifiable Information (PII). Jonah is a pricing and merchandising tool; it does not use Amazon's restricted PII roles, and it does not collect buyer names, addresses, emails, or order-level personal data.

3. How we use the data

We use Amazon data solely to provide the Service you requested — diagnosing the constraint on each listing, generating pricing/advertising/inventory recommendations, and (only where you authorize it) applying approved changes back to your Amazon account. We do not sell Amazon data, use it for advertising to third parties, or use it for any purpose unrelated to the Service.

4. Website & account information

When you join our waitlist or create an account, we collect your email address and basic account details to communicate with you and operate the Service. Our website uses essential cookies only; we do not run invasive third-party tracking.

5. How we store & protect data

  • Encryption — data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest.
  • Access control — least-privilege access; only authorized personnel and systems can access Amazon data, and access is logged.
  • Infrastructure — hosted on reputable cloud providers with industry-standard security controls.
  • Tokens & credentials — Amazon authorization tokens are stored encrypted and are never exposed to client-side code.
  • Monitoring & incident response — we monitor for unauthorized access and will notify affected users and Amazon of any qualifying security incident as required.

6. Sharing & sub-processors

We do not sell or rent your data. We share data only with sub-processors strictly necessary to operate the Service, under contractual confidentiality and data-protection terms:

  • Cloud hosting & database (e.g., [AWS / your provider]) — storage and compute.
  • AI model providers (e.g., [Anthropic / OpenAI]) — to generate explanations and recommendations; only the minimum necessary, non-identifying operational data is sent.
  • Market data (e.g., Keepa) — competitor price/history context.

A current list of sub-processors is available on request.

7. Data retention & deletion

We retain Amazon data only while your account is active and you remain connected. You may disconnect Jonah from your Amazon account at any time. On disconnection or on a verified deletion request, we delete or anonymize your Amazon Selling Partner data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. You can also request deletion at support@getjonah.com.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data (e.g., under GDPR/CCPA). Contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; material changes will be posted here with a revised "Last updated" date.
