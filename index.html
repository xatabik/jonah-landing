The method · Goldratt's five focusing steps

Diagnose before you prescribe.

Jonah runs the Theory of Constraints on every ASIN, around the clock. Find the bottleneck. Wring everything from it cheaply. Only then spend.

1

Identify the constraint

Walk the funnel — impressions, CTR, CVR, margin, inventory — and pinpoint the single stage throttling profit on this listing.

2

Exploit it — for free first

Get maximum profit from the constraint with zero spend: cut wasteful bids, fix the price, tune the offer.

3

Subordinate everything else

Jonah's don't-do list keeps you from throwing ad budget at a conversion problem.

4

Elevate the constraint

Once the free wins are banked, invest deliberately — scale the ads, improve the listing, push the rank.

5

Repeat — the constraint moves

Break one bottleneck and another appears. Jonah watches continuously and re-diagnoses, so inertia never becomes the constraint.
